Chess C# Client

This is the root of your AI. Stay out of the 

Joueur/
folder, it does most of the heavy lifting to play on our game servers. Your AI, and the game objects it manipulates are all in 
Games/Chess/
, with your very own AI living in 
Games/Chess/ai.cs
for you to make smarter.
